# IAConsult-Labs

## 🚀 AI & SaaS Engineering for Africa

IAConsult-Labs is a digital transformation and AI engineering initiative based in Niger, focused on building scalable SaaS platforms, intelligent automation systems, and business-grade CRM solutions tailored for African markets.

---

## 🌍 Our Mission

To empower African businesses, NGOs, institutions, and entrepreneurs through:

- Intelligent automation
- Multi-tenant SaaS systems
- Custom CRM platforms
- AI-powered assistants
- Secure and scalable cloud infrastructure

---
